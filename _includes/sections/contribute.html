Call for Papers

Selected Contributions

All the contributions will be presented during the workshop in two different poster sessions. The authors will provide an overview of their works during the spotlight presentation session.

Poster Session 1 - Morning

  • M. Bauza and A. Rodriguez, Tactile Localization for unknown and known objects.
  • N. Doshi and A. Rodriguez, Hybrid Differential Dynamic Programming for Planar Manipulation Primitives.
  • J. Bowkett, J. Burdick, and R. Detry, Visual Extraction Effort Estimation for Grasp Selection Among Unstructured Massime Objects.
  • F. Bottarel, G. Vezzani, U. Pattacini, and L. Natale, GRASPA 1.0: GRASPA is a Robot Arm grasPing Performance benchmArk.
  • C. Gabellieri, F. Angelini, V. Arapi, A. Palleschi, M. G. Catalano, G. Grioli, L. Pallottino, A. Bicchi, M. Bianchi, and M. Garabini, Grasp It like a Pro: Grasp of Unknown Objects with Robotic Hands based on Skilled Human Expertise.

Poster Session 2 - Afternoon

  • W. Bejjani, M. R. Dogar, and M. Leonetti, Heuristic Learning for Look-Ahead Manipulation Planning in Continuous Action Space.
  • G. Solak and L. Jamone, Learning object-centric trajectories of dexterous hand manipulation tasks from demonstration.
  • A. Melnik, L. Lach, M. Plappert, T. Korthals, R. Haschke, and H. Ritter, Tactile Sensing and Deep Reinforcement Learning for In-Hand Manipulation Tasks.
  • J. Pankert and M. Hutter Whole-Body Kinematic Model Predicitve Control for Continuous Mobile Manipulation Tasks.
  • Y. Peng, J. Huang, and M. Cakmak, Horizontal Multi-Surface Random Sample Consensus for Robust Customizable Shelf Perception.
